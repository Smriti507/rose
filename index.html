SMOKED PORK

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt & spices as needed

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until soft.
  4. Add pork, cover and cook until tender.
  5. Serve hot with rice.

VEGETABLE BIRYANI

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp oil / ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add vegetables, tomatoes, and spices, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix soaked rice and water, cook until done.
  5. Serve hot with raita.

RASMALAI

Ingredients:

  • 6–8 rasgullas
  • 1 liter milk
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp chopped dry fruits
  • 2–3 cardamom pods
  • A few saffron strands

Instructions:

  1. Boil milk until it thickens slightly.
  2. Add sugar, saffron, and cardamom, mix well.
  3. Squeeze rasgullas gently to remove syrup.
  4. Dip them into the thickened milk.
  5. Garnish with dry fruits and chill before serving.

PIZZA SANDWICH

Ingredients:

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum, onion, tomato
  • 1 tsp chili flakes & oregano
  • Butter for toasting

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter and pizza sauce on bread slices.
  2. Add vegetables and cheese as filling.
  3. Sprinkle chili flakes and oregano.
  4. Cover with another bread slice.
  5. Toast or grill until golden and crispy.
